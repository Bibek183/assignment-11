About Aquaman

Aquaman, aka Arthur Curry, is the half-human, half-Atlantean ruler of the underwater kingdom of Atlantis. Born to a lighthouse keeper and the queen of Atlantis, Arthur possesses superhuman abilities and the power to communicate with marine life. Aquaman made his first comic appearance in More Fun Comics #73 (1941).
