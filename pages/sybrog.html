About Cyborg

Cyborg, aka Victor Stone, is a superhero with a cybernetic body that combines human ingenuity with advanced technology. After a tragic accident, his father rebuilt him using alien tech, giving him superhuman strength, speed, and advanced weaponry integrated into his body.

Cyborg debuted in DC Comics #26 (1980) and is a core member of the Justice League and Teen Titans.

His story explores themes of humanity, technology, and identity in a futuristic world.
