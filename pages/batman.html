About Batman

Batman, aka Bruce Wayne, witnessed the tragic loss of his parents in Gotham City and vowed to fight crime. Trained to physical and mental perfection, he uses his wealth, detective skills, and advanced gadgets to protect the city from villains. Batman made his first comic appearance in Detective Comics #27 (1939).

The first major Batman movie was "Batman" (1989), starring Michael Keaton. Since then, Batman has appeared in various films:

  • 1989–1992: Michael Keaton in 2 films
  • 1995–1997: Val Kilmer and George Clooney in "Batman Forever" and "Batman & Robin"
  • 2005–2012: Christian Bale in "The Dark Knight Trilogy"
  • 2016–2023: Ben Affleck and Robert Pattinson in modern adaptations

Each adaptation brought a new vision of the hero, evolving his personality, struggles, and moral choices to fit modern times.
