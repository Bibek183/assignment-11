About Black Adam

Black Adam, born Teth-Adam, was a champion chosen by the wizard Shazam in ancient Kahndaq. Empowered by the gods, he sought to protect his people but was eventually corrupted by his own power and desire for vengeance. After centuries of imprisonment, he returns to the modern world, determined to enforce his own form of justice â€” often clashing with both heroes and villains.

Black Adam made his first comic appearance in The Marvel Family #1 (1945). Known for his complex morality, he has been portrayed as both a ruthless villain and a reluctant protector.

In live-action, Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson brought the character to life in the 2022 film "Black Adam", expanding his presence in the DC cinematic universe.
