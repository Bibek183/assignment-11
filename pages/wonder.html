About Wonder Woman

Wonder Woman, also known as Princess Diana of Themyscira, is a warrior of the Amazons and one of the most iconic superheroes in the DC Universe. Gifted with superhuman abilities by the Greek gods, she is a symbol of truth, justice, and equality. She wields the Lasso of Truth, indestructible bracelets, and a god-killer sword.

Wonder Woman made her first appearance in All Star Comics #8 (1941) and quickly became a feminist icon and a champion for peace. She often bridges the gap between humanity and the mythical world of the Amazons.

Gal Gadot portrays Wonder Woman in the DC Extended Universe, bringing the character to life in films such as "Wonder Woman" (2017) and "Wonder Woman 1984" (2020).
