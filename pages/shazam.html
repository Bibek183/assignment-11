About Shazam

Shazam, formerly known as Captain Marvel, is a superhero empowered by ancient magic. When Billy Batson says the magic word "SHAZAM," he transforms into an adult superhero with the powers of six legendary figures: Solomon, Hercules, Atlas, Zeus, Achilles, and Mercury.

Shazam was first introduced in Whiz Comics #2 (1940) and continues to be a fan favorite for his blend of magic and youthful spirit.
