About Superman

Superman, aka Kal-El, was sent to Earth from the dying planet Krypton. Raised as Clark Kent in Smallville, he discovered his superpowers and later chose to use them to protect humanity. Superman made his first comic appearance in Action Comics #1 (1938).

The first major Superman movie was "Superman: The Movie" (1978), starring Christopher Reeve. Since then, Superman has appeared in various films:

  • 1980–1987: Christopher Reeve as Superman in 4 films
  • 2006: Brandon Routh in "Superman Returns"
  • 2013–2023: Henry Cavill in "Man of Steel", "Batman v Superman", and "Justice League"

Each adaptation brought a new vision of the hero, evolving his personality, struggles, and moral choices to fit modern times.
