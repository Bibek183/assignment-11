About Green Lantern

Green Lantern, most notably Hal Jordan, is a member of the intergalactic Green Lantern Corps, wielding a power ring that creates constructs fueled by willpower. The ring allows him to fight evil and protect the universe.

First appearing in All-American Comics #16 (1940), Green Lantern has become a symbol of courage and determination.

Green Lantern has appeared in animated series and movies, and the live-action movie starred Ryan Reynolds as Hal Jordan.
